Our Story

Founded in 2015, Jonaj Furnitures began as a small family workshop in Kiambu County, Kenya. What started as a passion project crafting custom pieces for neighbors has grown into a respected furniture brand serving clients across the region. Our founder, John Elias, combined traditional woodworking techniques with modern design principles to create furniture that stands the test of time.

Today, we maintain that same dedication to quality while expanding our capabilities to serve more customers. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the same attention to detail and commitment to excellence that defined our first creations.

Our Mission

At Jonaj Furnitures, we exist to transform houses into homes through thoughtfully designed, expertly crafted furniture. We believe that quality furniture should be accessible, sustainable, and built to last for generations.

We're committed to:

  • Using responsibly sourced materials from local suppliers
  • Employing and training skilled Kenyan artisans
  • Reducing waste through efficient production methods
  • Creating timeless designs that never go out of style

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do:

  • Quality Craftsmanship: We take pride in every joint, finish, and detail
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your happiness is our ultimate measure of success
  • Sustainability: We protect Kenya's natural resources for future generations
  • Community Focus: We grow by helping our community grow with us
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our designs and techniques

Meet Our Artisans

Behind every Jonaj piece is a team of skilled craftspeople who pour their expertise into every creation. We invest in ongoing training and fair wages to maintain Kenya's rich woodworking traditions while embracing modern techniques.
